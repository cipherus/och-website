WHAT IS THIS EVENT ABOUT?

This event is about turning a museum into a place of creativity and creation. Our dream is to motivate new people to join the hack community and to provide an event where young hackers can work on their dreams of their own little pet projects, while experienced hackers can compete for prices in ambitious categories. We want to unite participants from all kinds of backgrounds. We designers, organizers and artists to join the community of developers. We just want to people to unfold all the creativity they have in an inspiring environment.


WHAT IS THE ZKM?

The ZKM is a modern musuem for art and media, that presents a unique mixture of wonderful architecture and refreshing new works of art. The current exhibition is called opencodes and shows a nice selection of artifacts that layered the ground for our daily digital world. Opencodes is always open for the public and free of charge. It presents a unique atmosphere for working and getting inspired. A perfect place for a hackathon!


WHO ARE WE?

We're a German student-run nonprofit organisation focused all around coding and hacking. We try to bring a bit of silicon valley to the german computer-science and start-up community by disrupting the status quo. We do this by organising hackathons, coding boot camps and other similar events. Our team consists of students from a variety of backgrounds: Computer Science and Electrical Engineering, Economics, Business, and more.
