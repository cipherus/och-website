About

With around 200 participants, the OpenCodes Hackathon is the largest student-run hackathon in Germany and thus one of the largest events of its kind in Europe.

There are many things that distinguish us from others, but the most notable is our close collaboration with the ZKM, a museum which the New York Times dubbed as an institution that has no exact parallel in the world and not even a recognizable echo in America. Together with the ZKM, the OpenCodes Hackathon offers an unprecedented combination of technology and creativity. We bring designers and artists together with computer scientists and engineers to create truly ingenious projects.

The OpenCodes Hackathon will be held for two days as part of Open Codes - Living in Digital Worlds, an exceptional tech exhibit by the ZKM. Surrounded by impressive works of art and astonished museum visitors, the general public will have an unprecedented opportunity to experience a hackathon in person. It will give those who dare a small glimpse into the world of digital transformation, a world that changes our lives every day.

Hack & Söhne is part of talKIT, the largest student organized technology forum in the German-speaking countries. This means that we can draw on eight years of experience in the area of project management. We know how to successfully organize large events.
