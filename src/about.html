What are we trying to achieve?

It's about turning a museum into a place of creation and innovation. We want to motivate people to join the hacking community and provide an event where young hackers can work on their own pet projects, while experienced hackers can compete for prizes in ambitious categories.

We want to unite participants from all kinds of backgrounds. We want designers, organizers and artists to join the community of makers and hackers. We want people to pour all their creativity into their project in an inspiring environment.

WHO ARE WE?

We're a German student-run nonprofit organisation focused all around coding and hacking. We try to bring a bit of silicon valley to the german computer-science and start-up community by disrupting the status quo. We do this by organising hackathons, coding boot camps and other similar events. Our team consists of students from a variety of backgrounds: Computer Science and Electrical Engineering, Economics, Business, and more.
