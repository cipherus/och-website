Okay, let's get to the point.
Our trademarks at Hack & Söhne are diversity, collaboration and low entry barriers. With the categories we present here, we want to make this vision a reality.
This is why our "Hack & Söhne" main category is totally free of limitations and guidelines. Whatever you have in mind will certainly fit into this category.
For those looking to solve more specific problems there are the three categories powered by our partners.
Read more below!

How can I win?

In the "Hack & Söhne" category we'll provide an elaborate evaluation process with a jury of seven professionals from various fields. Read more about the Jury and the selection process in the jury section.

Do I have to compete?

No and Yes, or Yes and No.
We don't force you to take part in the competition, but we encourage everyone to take the chance, because we believe that each and every participant has a chance to win, whether professional hacker or first-timer. It's important for us to note that it doesn't have to be your goal to win prizes.
Our driving motivation is for you to enjoy this hackathon and have a great time. So in the end it's up to you, how you spend your time. Just make yourself comfortable and we're sure, that you'll create awesome stuff!
