    09:00 - 11:00 Arrival - Registration - Breakfast

    The official start of the event is at 11am with the Opening Session. If you happen to be early however, we got your covered. Take your time to check out the amazing location, get your goodies bag and enjoy some fresh coffee with a little breakfast before we start off into the Hackathon.

    11:00 - 11:45 Opening Session - official start

    Now it's time to kick off the event. We know, you're not here to listen to random strangers talking about unimportant stuff, so we'll keep it as short as possible! In these 45 minutes, we'll tell you everything you need to know to have a perfect hackathon. What are the categories? What the prizes? Where can I ask questions? Who are the sponsors? Where are the toilets? No matter what, we'll cover it all.... and if not, just hit up one of us in the red hoodies.

    presented by : Hack & Söhne
    11:45 - 13:00 Teambuilding

    You don't have team yet or you have a team, but no idea even both? No problem, we've arranged this team-building phase ensure everyone can get a perfect hackathon experience. There'll be a blackboard where you can pin your idea an search for members and, supported with some snacks and drinks you'll be able to get in touch with everyone who is looking for a team. You are not the only one - trust me on this one.

    support by: Hack & Söhne
